Frequently Asked Questions

What is your return policy? +

We offer a 100-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Returns are free for orders over $100. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return.

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping: 3-7 business days. Express shipping: 1-3 business days. International shipping: 7-14 business days depending on destination. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

Do you offer international shipping? +

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination. All international orders may be subject to customs duties and taxes, which are the responsibility of the recipient.

How do I care for my ELEVATE garments? +

Each product comes with specific care instructions. Generally, we recommend cold water wash, gentle cycle, and line drying to maintain quality and longevity. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners. For wool and cashmere items, dry cleaning is recommended.

What makes ELEVATE sustainable? +

We use ethically sourced materials (organic cotton, recycled polyester, sustainable wool), carbon-neutral shipping, eco-friendly packaging, fair labor practices, and 1% of all sales goes to environmental causes. Our entire supply chain is transparent and audited annually.
