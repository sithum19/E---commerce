Our Story

Crafting premium menswear with purpose, sustainability, and timeless design at the forefront of everything we do.

Redefining Modern Menswear

Founded in 2015, ELEVATE was born from a simple idea: clothing should make you look good, feel good, and do good. We saw a gap in the market for premium menswear that didn't compromise on ethics, sustainability, or craftsmanship.

What started as a small studio in New York has grown into a global brand, but our core mission remains unchanged: to create exceptional clothing that stands the test of time, both in style and durability.

Our Journey

2015

The Beginning

ELEVATE was founded in a small Brooklyn studio by fashion industry veterans James Wilson and Sarah Chen. Their vision: create premium menswear that blends timeless design with modern sustainability.

2017

First Collection Launch

Launched our debut collection featuring 12 essential pieces. The collection sold out within 48 hours, confirming there was strong demand for ethically-made premium menswear.

2019

Sustainability Commitment

Made our full transition to sustainable materials and ethical manufacturing. Became a certified B Corporation and launched our carbon-neutral shipping initiative.

2021

Global Expansion

Expanded to 25 countries and opened our flagship store in New York. Launched our recycling program where customers can return worn items for recycling and receive store credit.

2023

Today & Beyond

Now serving over 50,000 customers worldwide with 100+ SKUs. Continuing to innovate with new sustainable materials and circular design principles while maintaining our commitment to exceptional craftsmanship.

Our Values

Sustainability First

Every decision we make considers environmental impact. From material sourcing to packaging, we prioritize the planet without compromising on quality.

Ethical Craftsmanship

We partner with artisans and manufacturers who share our commitment to fair wages, safe working conditions, and respect for traditional techniques.

Timeless Design

We create pieces meant to last for years, not just seasons. Our designs focus on versatility, quality, and enduring style over fleeting trends.

Customer Obsession

Your satisfaction is our priority. From our 100-day return policy to personalized style consultations, we're committed to exceptional service at every touchpoint.

Continuous Innovation

We're constantly exploring new sustainable materials, production methods, and design approaches to push the boundaries of what's possible in fashion.

Transparency

We believe in radical transparency. From pricing to production, we're open about our processes, costs, and challenges.
