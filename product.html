Care Instructions

To ensure your blazer maintains its premium quality and longevity, please follow these care instructions:

Do

  • Dry clean only
  • Use wooden hangers
  • Store in breathable garment bag
  • Brush with clothes brush after wear
  • Steam to remove wrinkles

Don't

  • Machine wash or tumble dry
  • Use wire hangers
  • Store in plastic bags
  • Iron directly on fabric
  • Use bleach or harsh chemicals
